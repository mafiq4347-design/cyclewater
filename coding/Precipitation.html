🌧️ Precipitation

The critical stage that returns water back to Earth's surface.

Phase: Clouds to Surface

What is Precipitation?

Precipitation is the process where water **falls from the atmosphere** back down to the Earth's surface.

Depending on the local air temperature, this water can reach us in various forms, including liquid rain or frozen states like snow, sleet, or hail.

The Precipitation Process

1

Clouds become saturated with water.

2

Droplets combine & grow heavier.

3

Cloud cannot hold the weight.

4

Water falls to Earth.

5

Returns to water bodies.

Key Facts

  • Includes rain, snow, sleet, hail.
  • Triggered when clouds are too heavy.
  • Essential for Earth's life.

Did You Know?

Not all precipitation is just rain! In cold climates, water changes its state to solid (snow or ice) before reaching the ground, all depending on the temperature of the air it passes through.
